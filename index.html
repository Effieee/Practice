Why Wellory Health

Affordable nutrition care at little to no cost

Under the Affordable Care Act, all insurance carriers cover the cost of nutrition counseling for those at-risk of chronic disease.

That means you’ll get the quality care you deserve, with minimal out-of-pocket costs.In fact, most of our insured clients pay $0!

How it works

Step 1

Find your in-network provider

We’ll find dietitians who are in-network with your insurance so that you can save money on the quality care you deserve.

Step 2

Meet with your dietitian

Using telehealth visits, you’ll meet with your dietitian from the comfort of your own home.

Step 3

Build a plan that works for you

Your dietitian will help you build a custom nutrition plan, and help you make progress with ongoing visits.
